About Me

As a Developer

The pandemic lockdown back in 2020 has led me to discover the world of coding. Being a self-driven individual, I was constantly searching for ideas and ways to make the most out of my time being at home and so I decided to try my hands at coding and of course being unsure of where to start, I got myself dipping my toe in the water by first self-learning Python online as my first introductory experience into the programming world.

When I came across a local coding bootcamp and enrolled on their online program on a part-time basis, I was introduced to the world of software development and Javascript and thus fast-forward today, I've learnt to build a wide range of projects and software applications, gradually honing my coding skills and understanding along the way.

Why coding fascinates me? - Because I've always been a builder at heart. I am extremely passionate about building things from houses to woodwork, miniature models, 3d printing, handicrafts, to Lego, and so on. Hence having discovered the resemblance between building physical objects with digital products, in addition to the hours invested on learning code everyday, I've made a firm decision to take on this path as my career moving forward.
